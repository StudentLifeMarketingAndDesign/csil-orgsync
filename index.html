Our Services

The UCS offers a variety of counseling services. All of the services are available to currently registered students. Nonstudents are eligible for a one-session consultation and possibly referral. Nonstudents are also welcome to use our program groups on a space available basis. Except for selected tests, our services are provided without charge. The purpose of your initial interview with a UCS staff member will be to determine which service(s) best suit your individual needs. Our staff represents many aspects of the diversity of the campus and has a commitment to meeting the special needs of individuals from various backgrounds.
